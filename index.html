About Us

THE PECKER PLACE

The Pecker Place is a poultry farm company based in Bungoma, Kenya. The company was founded in 2023 by Giovanni Bwayo with the goal of providing high-quality poultry products to the local community. The company currently raises chickens only and is committed to providing its customers with healthy, fresh poultry products that are raised in a humane and sustainable manner.

We are a small-scale poultry farm company that currently operates on an acre plot of land in Bungoma, Kenya with a capacity of raising 200 chickens. We believe that everyone deserves to have access to high-quality poultry products, and we are dedicated to making that happen.
